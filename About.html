Drip Irrigation

Drip irrigation is an efficient method of watering plants and crops by delivering water directly to the root zone of individual plants or through a network of tubing, pipes, and emitters. It minimizes water wastage by delivering water directly to the plant's root zone.

Drip irrigation helps conserve water resources, making it an environmentally friendly option, especially in areas with water scarcity or restrictions. By delivering water gently to the soil surface, drip irrigation minimizes soil erosion compared to traditional surface watering methods.
