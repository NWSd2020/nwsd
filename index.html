A Story
About Us

The idea of this device came from an incident that happened at delhi on 16 december 2012 of Nirbhaya case, from that moment we realized to help women and stop these incidents. After a long research we have come up with this Women Safety Device. The main reason to start our company is to reduce the attacks on women that are happening in our society. Many more incidents are happening after the Nirbhaya case so, inorder to reduce these attacks we have implemented a women safety device. Using this device women can save themselves from any situation like when they're travelling alone this device is very much helpful to protect themselves from attackers. The working of this device includes sharing thier location to friends and family from their contact list.

This invention relates to the field of Artificial intelligence and nano technology . Still further the invention relates to that of women safety . Still further, the invention relates to that of a safeguarding self protection device a woman can wear to shield from potential attackers. Still furthermore, the invention relates to a protective device to be worn by a woman which has also features of voice recognition enabled in it.

Mission

Our mission is to build a device for women safety where they can save themselves from the attackers.It is a compatible and user-friendly device.This device works independently without any mobile connectivity.This device can add much better protection to every women.This is a budjet-friendly and customized device. This device help women to come out from any sudden dangers or harassments.

Vision

Our vision is to ensure women safety and protect them from the atrocities that's happening in our society with the help of this IOT device. Women teasing and harassment in public transport can be drastically reduced. Women harassment can be detected in sparse traffic resign. Women confidence will automatically get boosted because of our device and they can do their work independently.

Goals

Our major goal is to reduce the attrocities faced by the women and to reach this device to every women out there. Using this device every women can protect themselves from any type of attacks like harrasments,sexual attacks and kidnaps,etc. Women Confidence will automatically get boosted Because of our device and they can do their work independently. The main motto of our device is-inorder to make women feel real freedom.

we are giving the solution using our device

Our device helps women when someone tries to attack them as well as she gives single click information to her friends and family members or the police. She can also give an electric shock to the person who is going to attack her. If the person throws the device, it will alert you based on the words coming out of her mouth(EX:HELP,HELP ME,ETC…) and transfer the live location, message AND call to the police. IN This way women can be protected from THE ATTACKER.

03

how to use our device

Firstly every women must carry this device with them. If they are facing any trouble any where then they should press the push button in the device.Then the information is sent to the nearby police station , family and friends. Then the police tracks the location of the women ,arrest the attacker and saves the women. In another case, if the women can't take the device out of her bag she can shout loud like help me, save me using the keyword Nirbhaya. Then the device recognises her voice and sends the information to the emergency contact list .
